Welcome to the Virat Kohli Tribute Page

Welcome to the tribute page dedicated to Virat Kohli, the Indian cricketer and former captain of the Indian national team. Explore his biography and achievements to learn more about his remarkable journey in cricket.

Biography

Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer and former captain of the Indian national team. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen in the world.

Kohli has received numerous awards for his performances including the Sir Garfield Sobers Trophy for ICC Cricketer of the Year in 2017 and 2018. He holds several records including the fastest century by an Indian cricketer in ODIs and the fastest to reach 8,000, 9,000, 10,000, 11,000, and 12,000 runs in One Day Internationals.

Outside of cricket, Kohli is known for his philanthropic work and has been involved in various charitable initiatives.

Achievements

Some of Virat Kohli's achievements include:

  • ICC Cricketer of the Year: 2017, 2018
  • Fastest century by an Indian cricketer in ODIs
  • Fastest to reach 8,000, 9,000, 10,000, 11,000, and 12,000 runs in One Day Internationals
